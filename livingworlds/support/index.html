FAQ

Why are the images portrait when Joseph's demo is landscape?

Note that Joseph's demo actually uses modified versions of the original art; Wizards of the Coast commissioned updates and expansions to a few of the images for their game battle mage, and those are the images Joseph used. There isn't a complete set of 12 months in that size, however, nor are any of the overlays made for that size. Because of that, and to as faithfully as possible re-create the original Seize the Day worlds, we're using the original graphics.
